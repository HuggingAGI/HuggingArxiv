> ARPA: A Novel Hybrid Model for Advancing Visual Word Disambiguation Using Large Language Models and Transformers

# 摘要

> 在自然语言处理与计算机视觉的快速发展中，视觉词义消歧（VWSD）任务显得尤为关键与挑战。我们迫切需要能够无缝融合并解读多模态数据的模型。设想一个系统，它既能深入细致地理解语言，又能同时解读周围世界的丰富视觉信息。我们推出的ARPA架构，巧妙结合了大型语言模型的深度上下文理解与transformer的高效特征提取，再通过定制的图神经网络（GNN）层，深入挖掘数据中的复杂关系与微妙差异。这一创新架构不仅在视觉词义消歧领域树立了新标杆，更通过其多功能框架，巧妙融合语言与视觉数据，确保在复杂消歧场景中的稳健表现。实验与比较分析进一步证实了我们模型的显著优势，预示着其在该领域引领新标准的潜力。此外，通过精细的数据增强与多模态训练，ARPA在实验中表现卓越。ARPA的问世，为视觉词义消歧领域带来了突破性进展，有效弥合了语言与视觉之间的鸿沟。我们诚邀各界专家深入探索ARPA的无限可能，共同开创人工智能的新纪元。

> In the rapidly evolving fields of natural language processing and computer vision, Visual Word Sense Disambiguation (VWSD) stands as a critical, yet challenging task. The quest for models that can seamlessly integrate and interpret multimodal data is more pressing than ever. Imagine a system that can understand language with the depth and nuance of human cognition, while simultaneously interpreting the rich visual context of the world around it.
  We present ARPA, an architecture that fuses the unparalleled contextual understanding of large language models with the advanced feature extraction capabilities of transformers, which then pass through a custom Graph Neural Network (GNN) layer to learn intricate relationships and subtle nuances within the data. This innovative architecture not only sets a new benchmark in visual word disambiguation but also introduces a versatile framework poised to transform how linguistic and visual data interact by harnessing the synergistic strengths of its components, ensuring robust performance even in the most complex disambiguation scenarios. Through a series of experiments and comparative analysis, we reveal the substantial advantages of our model, underscoring its potential to redefine standards in the field. Beyond its architectural prowess, our architecture excels through experimental enrichments, including sophisticated data augmentation and multi-modal training techniques.
  ARPA's introduction marks a significant milestone in visual word disambiguation, offering a compelling solution that bridges the gap between linguistic and visual modalities. We invite researchers and practitioners to explore the capabilities of our model, envisioning a future where such hybrid models drive unprecedented advancements in artificial intelligence.
