# TrojanRobot：针对物理世界中机器人操控的后门攻击

发布时间：2024年11月18日

`LLM应用` `机器人` `人工智能`

> TrojanRobot: Backdoor Attacks Against Robotic Manipulation in the Physical World

# 摘要

> 机器人操作指的是机器人运用机器人技术和人工智能的先进手段，自主地处理和与物体互动。大型语言模型（LLMs）及大型视觉语言模型（LVLMs）等强大工具的出现，大大提升了这些机器人在环境感知和决策方面的能力。然而，这些智能体的引入带来了诸如越狱攻击和对抗性攻击等安全威胁。
    在此次研究中，我们更进了一步，提出了一种专门针对机器人操作的后门攻击，并且首次在现实世界中实施了这种攻击。通过将后门视觉语言模型嵌入机器人系统的视觉感知模块，在常见物品作为触发条件时，我们成功误导了现实世界中机械臂的操作。在现实世界中的实验评估证实了所提出的后门攻击的有效性。

> Robotic manipulation refers to the autonomous handling and interaction of robots with objects using advanced techniques in robotics and artificial intelligence. The advent of powerful tools such as large language models (LLMs) and large vision-language models (LVLMs) has significantly enhanced the capabilities of these robots in environmental perception and decision-making. However, the introduction of these intelligent agents has led to security threats such as jailbreak attacks and adversarial attacks.
  In this research, we take a further step by proposing a backdoor attack specifically targeting robotic manipulation and, for the first time, implementing backdoor attack in the physical world. By embedding a backdoor visual language model into the visual perception module within the robotic system, we successfully mislead the robotic arm's operation in the physical world, given the presence of common items as triggers. Experimental evaluations in the physical world demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed backdoor attack.
